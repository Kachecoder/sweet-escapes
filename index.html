Project Overview

Sweet Escapes By Kiya is a luxury picnic and event planning service looking to enhance their operations and customer experience through a sophisticated e-commerce platform. The business requires a solution that reflects their premium brand positioning while streamlining bookings, payments, and customer engagement.

Project Objectives

  • Develop a high-end e-commerce platform (web & mobile) for Sweet Escapes By Kiya
  • Implement AI-driven personalization and automation features
  • Streamline booking, payment, and event planning processes
  • Enhance marketing capabilities and customer engagement
  • Create a luxury aesthetic that aligns with the brand's positioning

  • Feature Engineering

  • Output Generation
